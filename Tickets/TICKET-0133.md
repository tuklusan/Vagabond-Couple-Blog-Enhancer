# TICKET-0133: step6/step10 fabricated an entire fictional road trip when origin/destination came back empty
Status: closed
Priority: Critical
Type: Bug
Created: 2026-07-03
Description: 0127/0129/0131 hardened step1_title and step7_route_summary_box against empty/equal origin-destination (context-extraction non-determinism), but step6_first_body_paragraph and step10_journey_significance had no such guard. On an arsenalna1 run where context extraction again returned origin='' destination='' (post_title also empty -- no h1, no schema in this source), the writer had literally nothing to anchor to and FABRICATED an entire fictional trip: step6 wrote 'we drove from Kyiv to Lviv via the Zhytomyr-Rivne corridor... overnight in a crumbling Soviet hotel and the detour to the tunnel of love near Klevan' (540km, invented waypoints, invented lodging, invented detour -- NONE of this exists anywhere in the source, which is about a metro station descent). step10 similarly fabricated 'The Georgian stretch of our overland route revealed a land that folded Silk Road memory into every monastery dome... Persian, Ottoman, and Russian strands... an ancient kingdom' -- also pure invention, worse than the earlier 'via Chernihiv' single-waypoint fabrication (0127) because it invented a whole narrative with specific, plausible-sounding false details. G2 Pass-1 correctly caught it ('paragraph about driving from Kyiv to Lviv suddenly appears... completely unrelated paragraph about Georgia'). FIX: (1) extracted shared _no_real_route(context) helper (renamed from step1's _title_single_location, now used by step1/step6/step10); (2) step6's writer prompt now REQUIRES grounding in the real subject/sections when no_route is true, explicitly forbidding inventing an origin/destination/drive/corridor; reviewer's FACTS criterion adjusted to match; (3) step10 similarly drops the 'connects to the wider overland expedition/Silk Road' thesis framing (which was itself an invitation to fabricate) when no_route, replacing it with 'reflect on THIS post's real subject/sections, do not invent a route/journey'; reviewer's FACTS criterion explicitly fails on an invented route/region/theme; (4) sequencer.context_extraction_node now has a last-resort deterministic fallback (context_extractor.title_from_url_slug()) -- when post_title/origin/destination all come back empty AND --current-url was supplied, derive a real, zero-hallucination-risk subject from the post's own URL slug (e.g. 'arsenalna-kyiv-worlds-deepest-metro-station' -> 'Arsenalna Kyiv Worlds Deepest Metro Station') rather than leaving nodes with nothing to ground on. Verify: re-run arsenalna1 (with and without --current-url), confirm step6/step10 output stays grounded in the real post subject regardless of what context_extraction returns.
